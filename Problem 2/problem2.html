My Morning Routine

  1. Wake up and wonder about my life.
  2. Check my cellphone to see what possible good news is happening in the world; once again, nothing new.
  3. Finally leave my bed to then take care of my little baby Manny.
  4. After he is taken care of, I go ahead and start to figure out what I am going to eat or do for myself.
  5. Once I figure that out, the beginning part of my day is complete.

    6. The most important step in this whole list is taking care of Manny. He is a living creature and he is unable to do anything without the assistance of a human.
